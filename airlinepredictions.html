How does it work?




Airfare tracker Learning algorithm Airfare predictions
Unfare tracks over 50 Million airfare points every single day across different domestic routes in India in its big data database. It then applies a machine learning algorithm over the airfare points to find the pattern or trend of the airfare movements. Based on this model, Airline Flight Prediction predicts the future airfare movements and recommends you to either buy immediately or wait for the fare drop.
